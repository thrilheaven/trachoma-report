Leaving no one behind

‘I had a painful burning and prickling in both my eyes and lots of tears, as if I had something in my eyes all the time.’

Chief Abdul-Rahman had trichiasis in both eyes. He was gradually becoming blind, a state that would haave been irreversible. He was told he could have an operation to end the pain. “I had a painful burning and prickling in both my eyes and lots of tears, as if I had something in my eyes all the time. I was really pleased to have found a solution. My vision is so much better now.“

‘Now I’ll be able to milk my two cows, fetch firewood and cook for my beautiful grand- daughter.’

“Adiyo wove and sold necklaces for eight months to pay for the transport to take her grandmother to the clinic. After the surgery” Cheposera sat up straight, beaming with delight. “Now I’ll be able to milk my two cows, fetch firewood and cook for my beautiful grand-daughter.”
