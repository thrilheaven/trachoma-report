Alliance for GET2020, April 2016

WHO Alliance for the Global Elimination of Trachoma by 2020

In 1996, the World Health Organization (WHO) created the Alliance for the Global Elimination of Trachoma by 2020 (GET2020) to mobilise resources and foster cooperation within a worldwide partnership of Member States, nongovernmental organisations and the private sector. The Alliance is the principal platform through which the trachoma community works to deliver WHO’s recommended SAFE strategy – an approach backed by the 1998 World Health Assembly resolution WHA51.11.
